Structural cost advantage

The AI pipeline under the Conductor cuts the cost of early-stage engineering roughly 5–15× versus an in-house bureau [order-of-magnitude estimate].

Portfolio logic

Interest alignment

An equity model a traditional bureau cannot copy without breaking its own economics. We earn together with the client.
