services & partnership model

We earn when your project grows

We work with no upfront payments and no cost to the client. We fulfil our obligations strictly on a contractual basis and within our capabilities.

EQuilibrium applies a hybrid strategy for delivering engineering projects: we take a share in the project at an early stage and, together with the client, carry it from scratch to design documentation, a patent, series production and real sales. The model is flexible — tuned to each client’s budget and risk appetite; specific terms are fixed at the entry point, at gate G0.

What we do

The full engineering cycle

🔎

Feasibility & concepts

Feasibility assessment, TRL, patent landscape, 3+ concepts and a trade-study with a justified selection.

📋

Specs & requirements

Technical specification and conditions per GOST, a matrix of measurable requirements with tolerances and traceability.

📐

Calculations & design docs

Strength, dynamics, thermal, fatigue life, precision; drawing cascade per ESKD, specifications, BOM, parametric geometry.

🏭

Manufacturing

Process, routing and operation cards, CAM strategy, tooling, inspection operations.

⚖️

IP & patenting

Invention/utility-model applications, FTO analysis, protection strategy — turning your share into a protected asset.

🧪

Prototype & testing

Prototype, V&V protocols, digital twin, requirement verification before series.

💹

Financial model & round

P&L/CF/NPV/IRR, valuation, pitch deck, data room, raising financing for the product.

🚀

Pilot & series

Pilot batch, FAT/SAT, cost calculation, series launch, go-to-market.

🛠️

Component import substitution

Reverse engineering, design docs and process to replace an imported component.

How the share is formed

Three contribution baskets

The project SPV’s cap table is built from three contributions. Shares are set at entry and earned in as the contribution is actually made — vesting by gates, not all at once.

🧠 Client-idea

The idea, domain expertise, understanding of the market and the customer.

⚙️ EQuilibrium-execution

Engineering, design docs, IP, carrying the project through all gates to a result.

💰 Investor-capital

Money for tooling, prototype, pilot. Funded at the SPV level.
