About me

Aslan KAA — KAGIROV ABDUL-HAKIM AKHMADOVICH

Born on September 7, 1970 in the Chechen-Ingush ASSR, in a country called the USSR.

Projects

Oil & gas extraction. Energy. Agricultural projects. Manufacturing. Economy. Finance. IT Solutions.

  • Vacuum-Pulse Impact (VPI) technology applied to the reservoir using the Enhanced Downhole Pump K-2 (USNK-2). The pump is designed to treat the bottom-hole zone of the formation in order to improve its filtration characteristics and increase the productivity of producing wells operated by deep sucker-rod pumps.

  • Flow Conversion System (SPP) technology employing innovative valves embedded in the production tubing to increase oil output and extend the natural-flow period of wells in fields with a high gas factor, as well as on wells suitable for transition from natural flow to artificial-lift production methods.
